
# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Overview
End-to-end exploratory data analysis (EDA) of e-commerce customer data using Python to understand purchasing behavior, revenue drivers, and customer engagement patterns.

---

## Key Objectives
- Analyze customer spending and purchase frequency
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Evaluate the impact of subscriptions, discounts, payment methods, and shipping types
- Derive actionable business insights from customer behavior

---

## Dataset
~3,900 e-commerce customers with demographic, transactional, and behavioral data including purchase amounts, categories, subscriptions, discounts, payment methods, and review ratings.

---

## Analysis Highlights
- Loyal and repeat customers drive the majority of revenue
- Subscribed customers contribute disproportionately higher value
- Targeted discounts can increase basket size without reducing revenue
- Clothing and Accessories show the strongest repeat demand
- Digital payments and express shipping correlate with higher engagement
- Age has minimal impact on average purchase value

---

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebook

---

## Outcome
The analysis demonstrates that customer behavior and engagement are stronger predictors of value than demographics, supporting retention-focused and data-driven business strategies.
